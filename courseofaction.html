Course of Action


The KLS GITs Orators Club was founded on 21 September 2019 with an aim ‘To enable an individual to become a better public speaker and a leader with effective communication skills and organizational skills’. The club is open for the first year, second year and third year students of core engineering branch (Aeronautical, Civil, Computer Science, Electronics & Communication, Electrical & Electronics, Information Science and Mechanical).


Schedule and Activity Plan:


  • The Club meets once in a week for two hours.
  • Every meeting will have a word of the week where the speakers are supposed to use that word in their speech.
  • In every meeting the club conducts Formal Speech which is followed by informal speech at the end. Formal Speech could include:
    • Ice Breaker (Self Introduction)
    • Organised Speech
    • Visual aid speech
    • Motivational Speech
    • Persuasive Speech etc.
  • Informal Speech (Impromptu Speech) will be pick and speak and will be open for anyone except the volunteers.
  • The club invites non-members as guests who can attend the meeting and watch the events. They can participate in pick and speak and enroll to be a member if they wish.
  • At the end of the weekly meeting, the Orator Master/s of the week will be declared by the evaluators based on the best performance.
  • A separate team has been appointed from the core members who are responsible for the Orator Club’s website design and website administration.



The role of the coordinators is as follows:


  • Faculty Coordinator (should be from Centre for Language and Communication Studies) will remain constant and is responsible for the overall smooth conduction and execution of the club’s event every week and also maintain the Orators Club file.
  • Faculty Coordinator will select the Student Coordinators either every semester or every year.
  • The Students Coordinators with the assistance of Faculty coordinator will appoint organizers/evaluators from the core members in every meeting. These organizers and evaluators however will keep changing every week.
  • Student Coordinators will be communicating with Faculty coordinators as well as the core members regarding any kind of notifications or announcements. The club has an official whats app group for all the communication among the members.
    • Student Coordinators will be responsible for conducting additional activities like debate competition, group discussions, special talks, etc. every semester.
  • They will also decide the word of the week and announce it at the end of the meeting.


Member’s Role:


  • Organisers: who will take the complete responsibility of the smooth conduction of the meeting (compering, and making all arrangements) and at the end assigning roles for the next week with the word for the week by consulting the faculty/student coordinator, taking attendance, introducing the guests of the week etc.
  • Evaluators :like General evaluator, Grammarian, Filler counter (Ah counter), Body language evaluator (Only for Formal Speech). At the end of the formal speech each evaluator will give a short report on the performance of the participants.
  • Participants: are those who will prepare and give the formal speech; and at the end of evaluators report they too can give valuable feedback or comment on other participant’s performance.
